Back Of Beyond Apothecary
An educational game about the traditional use of herbs, in both folk medicine and mysticism. The player learns about the identification and use of plants through repeat encounter and finding remedies/solutions for ailments/complaints of the townsfolk. The player is encouraged by learning-process triggered character interactions that expand on the story of the town and surrounding area.
The 3D and 2D assets used in the game were made by me in Blender and FireAlpaca, for the music I utilised the Unity Asset Store.
